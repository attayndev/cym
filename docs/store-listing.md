# Store listing copy — Call Your Mom

Brand rule: recognition before information. People, not software. Every
feature is a story about showing up. No "sync/manage/organize/CRM" language.
Claims guardrail: everything below exists and ships today.

## App Store (iOS)

- **Name** (30 ch): `Call Your Mom - CYM` *(listing name; revisit if "Call Your Mom" frees up)*
- **Subtitle** (30 ch): `Never lose touch by accident`
- **Promotional text** (170 ch):
  > We've all said "I'll call them next week." Call Your Mom quietly remembers the people who matter — and the moment that makes reaching out easy.
- **Keywords** (100 ch):
  `stay in touch,relationships,keep in touch,birthday,networking,follow up,contacts,reminder,family`
- **Description**:

  Relationships don't always end. Sometimes they simply go quiet.

  The friend you keep meaning to text. The mentor you never thanked. The
  investor you met once. The cousin who always calls first. Important
  relationships disappear one busy week at a time — not from a falling-out,
  just from drift.

  Call Your Mom is the memory layer for your relationships. It quietly
  remembers, so you can simply show up.

  IT REMEMBERS
  Where you met, what you talked about, what you promised — captured in ten
  seconds. Point the camera at a business card or a conference badge and the
  details land in a new contact, ready to check.

  IT NOTICES
  Birthdays. Job changes. Long silences. Promises coming due. Connect your
  inboxes and "last talked" stays true on its own — we read who and when,
  never what (headers only, enforced by your provider's permissions).

  IT NUDGES
  Not constantly. A few a day, only when reaching out actually means
  something. Never a guilt list. When the words won't come, a note is
  drafted in your voice with the context already worked in — you edit,
  you send, always from your own apps.

  "You met Sarah at CES. She was interviewing then. She just landed the
  job — now's the moment."

  YOUR NETWORK IS YOURS
  No ads, ever, on any tier. Your data is never sold. Export everything or
  delete your account any time, right in the app.

  Free forever: capture without limits, keep 10 relationships warm, 3
  drafted notes a month. Plus removes the caps and adds connected inboxes,
  automatic job-change radar, and unlimited drafts — founding price
  $14.99/month or $119.99/year with a 7-day free trial, locked in for as
  long as you stay. ($29.99/month after launch.)

  Why "Call Your Mom"? Because the hardest relationship to keep warm is the
  one that matters most. If it works for her, it'll work for everyone else.
